Target Audiance

Description of the Target Aundience:

The target audience for the Discover the Weather website is eveyrone who wants to learn or discover the weather of the locations provided. This could be anyone, but more likely a person of residence or one traveling through the listed locations.

Two Personas:

A great example of two personas for the target audience of this website could include Jackson and Hilda. Jackson is a young man who wants to go camping this weekend with some friends and wants to ensure that the weather in the area will be desirable concitions for their plans. Hilde is an older lady who enjoys watching the birds from her window. She would search the site to see if there is desirable weather the next day to watch birds.

List of Scenarios:

Two scenarios were listed above and three more could include the following: 1. Jason wanting to go scate boarding tomorrow and checking to see if there was a chance of rain. 2. Chloe just planted flowers in her garden and checks the site to see if it is going to rain to water her flowers. 3. Jake wants to go fishing and hopes for an overcast day but no rain. He checks to see what the weather situations are for his fishing spots.
